## 高度相關文獻摘要

### 1. Chiang et al. 2005 (PMID: 15733757) ⭐

**Better adherence to the guidelines during cardiopulmonary resuscitation through the provision of audio-prompts**

| 項目 | 內容 |
|------|------|
| **設計** | 兩階段研究（觀察期 + 介入期） |
| **場域** | 臨床急救（影片分析） |
| **介入** | Audio-prompts（語音提示） |
| **主要發現** | |
| Hands-off periods | 16.9±7.9s → 12.7±5.3s |
| Total hands-off time | 273±153s → 164±94s |
| Intubation <20s | 10% → 56.3% |
| **結論** | Audio-prompts 可顯著改善臨床 CPR guideline adherence |

**摘要原文**：
> The study examined ways to enhance quality assurance in resuscitation practice. When audio prompts were introduced in the intervention phase, significant improvements emerged. The intervention group demonstrated reduced hands-off periods during CPR and improved intubation efficiency. The authors concluded that "Audio-prompts can improve the adherence to current CPR guidelines in the clinical setting significantly."

### 2. Milander et al. 1995 (PMID: 7584749) ⭐

**Chest compression and ventilation rates during cardiopulmonary resuscitation: the effects of audible tone guidance**

| 項目 | 內容 |
|------|------|
| **設計** | 觀察研究 + 實驗室研究 |
| **對象** | 12 例院內心跳停止 + 41 名志願者 |
| **介入** | Audible tone guidance（聽覺音調引導） |
| **主要發現** | |
| Compression rate | 74±22 → 100±3 /min (p<0.01) |
| End-tidal CO₂ | 15±7 → 17±7 torr (p<0.01) |
| **結論** | Audible tones 可顯著提升壓胸頻率 |

**摘要原文**：
> Only two of twelve patients received compressions meeting guidelines; none achieved proper ventilation rates. In the lab, two-thirds of certified providers compressed outside standards without audio cues. With audible tone guidance, mean compression rates improved significantly. The authors conclude that "audible tones to guide chest compression resulted in significantly higher chest compression rates."
